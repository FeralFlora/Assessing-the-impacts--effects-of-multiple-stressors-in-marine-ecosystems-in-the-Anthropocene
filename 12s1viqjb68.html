4. Present drivers
A crucial step on the assessment procedure of GBR is to identify the drivers of change.  Drivers have been referred as underlying causes of change(either positive or negative) in the environment (Outlook Report, 2014).  A driver is a factor that creates stressors, and the stressors are the specific effects that cause stress and thus it is important  For the purposes of the "Reef 2050 Integrated Monitoring and Reporting Program" the Great Barrier Reef Marine Park Authority propose six drivers:
 
  1. Climate change
  2. Population growth
  3. Economic growth
  4. Technological developments
  5. Societal attitudes
  6. Governance systems
