The need for the development of an ecosystem based approach to the assessment of cumulative impacts from multiple stressors has been recognized and emphasized for decades \citep{Wrona1996}, but despite early recognition of this need, researcher still argue for the adaptation of an assessment approach with greater focus on the characteristics and dynamics of the affected biological receptors \citep{Segner2014}. This can be taken to indicate a certain academic inertia in the field, but of course these two recommendations, although similar in essence, differ in sophistication with the more recent one having a slew of relevant contemporary research to refer to.
